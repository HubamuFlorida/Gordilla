Here I wanted to optimize shipments because selling outside Spain is expensive. I developed logic to automatically decide how to send each order. The system looks at where the customer is from (if they are from France, Germany, etc.) and how much shipping costs. If it pays off for Amazon to send it (Fulfillment) paying their fee, it is done that way; if it is better to send it from our warehouse (Seller), we do it ourselves. It is basically a decision system to save logistical costs and improve the margin of each sale without having to think about it order by order.
